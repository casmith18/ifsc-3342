Resources and Rentals

The Ouachita State Park Marina offers rental boats, a bait and tackle shop, fishing supplies and fuel. 70 covered and uncovered boat slips are available, and boaters will find battery charging, mooring buoys and boat towing. Visitors may rent 16-foot fishing boats, an 18-foot waterskiing boat and party barges in a range of sizes. There are also recreational craft including pedal boats, water bikes and canoes for rent. Boat launch ramps are onsite, and for the convenience of boaters with their own rigs, boat ramps are located in Camp Area A.
